TaskTon
Organize your thoughts. Plan ahead.

Build your Tasks.

Create New Tasks and Subtasks

TaskTon is built around breaking work down into pieces you can actually finish. Create tasks for anything you need to do, then split them into smaller subtasks that keep you focused and moving forward. Each task can have a title, description, due date, and an optional list, making it easy to keep track of both quick reminders and long-term goals. As you complete subtasks, your progress is reflected automatically. When finishing them all, and the task is marked complete. Whether it's homework, work projects, or personal plans, TaskTon helps you turn overwhelming to-do lists into manageable steps.

List it All Down.

Organize Tasks and Subtasks through Lists

Lists help you group related tasks together so nothing gets lost. Create custom lists with names and colours to visually separate different areas of your life, such as work, school, or personal projects. Tasks can be assigned to a list or left unlisted, giving you full flexibility in how you organize things. Lists can be edited or deleted at any time, and task counts update automatically so you always know what needs attention. With clear structure and visual cues, TaskTon keeps your tasks organized without getting in your way.

View the Big Picture.

Sort it All Out

When your task list grows, TaskTon gives you the tools to stay in control. Sort tasks alphabetically, by due date, or by when they were created, in either ascending or descending order so you can focus on what matters most right now. Powerful filtering options let you quickly switch between all tasks, completed tasks, incomplete ones, today's tasks, or tasks from a specific list. This makes it easy to find the tasks to focus on and complete them ASAP. From a full dashboard view to focused daily planning, TaskTon helps you see the big picture without losing track of the details.
